### Theorem:

For every positive integer $n\geq 4,\; 2^n \geq n^2.$

<u>proof</u>: Let $P(n)$ be the predicate $2n\geq n^2$.

BASE CASE: $n=4$. $2^4 = 16 = 4^2 \checkmark$ so $P(4)$ is true.
