## Sets

Definition:
> A <u>set</u> is a collection of stuff. The stuff inside the set are called <u>elements</u>

e.g. A = {1,2,3,{4,5}}

The elements are the numbers 1,2,3 and the set {4,5}.

It is *not* correct to say that 4 is an element of A.
