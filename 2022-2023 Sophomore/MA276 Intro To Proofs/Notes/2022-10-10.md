### Definition
Let $A$ be a set. We saw that a family of sets $F\subseteq\mathcal{P}(A)$ is a <u>partition</u> of $A$ to mean:
1. $A = \bigcup F$
2. for all $X,Y \in F, X\cap Y \neq\emptyset \to X=Y$
