### Ch10  Brief Report Outline

#### Types of Brief Reports:
- Progress Reports
- White papers/briefings
	- Short summaries
	- Focused on selling something usually corporate
- Incident Reports
	- Something happened
	- Why it happened
	- How it was handled
	- How to prevent
- Lab Reports
	- Experimental procedure
	- results
	- discussion
	- illustrations as relevant
- Progress reports
- for managers (or that role)
- project status
- future activities
