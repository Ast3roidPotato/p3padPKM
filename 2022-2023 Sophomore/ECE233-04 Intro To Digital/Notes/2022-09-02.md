## Number Systems
- All numbers are represented using bases
	- Bases are also known as a "radix," r
	- Each digit **D** is restricted to 0 $\leq$ D $<$ r
- Systems we will be using:
	- Binary
	- Octal
	- Decimal
	- Hex
